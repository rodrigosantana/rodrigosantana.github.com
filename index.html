About me…

I am graduated inf Oceanography by the University of Vale do Itajaí in 2008, where I was congratulated with the academic merit due of his eminence in scientific researches and ethical attitudes. From 2003 to 2009 he worked as an adjunct researcher in the fishery study group of the University of Vale do Itajaí. During the same period I contributed to the brazillian scientific demersal fisheries managment committee with recommendations about deep-sea crabs fisheries and population dynamics.

During the years of 2009 and 2010 I acted as fisheries statistical manager of the Ministry of Fisheries and Aquaculture (MPA) where I aimed to develop and implement new statistical methods for monitoring fisheries catches and efforts. Recently, I was consultant for the Ibero-american States Organization (OEI) and Brazillian Ministry of Fisheries and Aquaculture (MPA) with the responsability to consolidate the national fish statistical of fisheries and aquaculture. I has experience in fisheries monitoring, statistical and geostatistical modelling applied to fisheries, as well it´s managments.

Actually, I am a grad student majoring in Environment Science and Technology in the University of Vale do Itajaí financed by the Federal State Governament of Brazil (CAPES), as well as been an undergrad student majoring in Web based Systems at the same University. Along with all that, I still work at the Albatroz Task Force/BirdLife International - Albatroz Project from Brazil were I am responsable by the statistical modelling analysis of the fishing fleet impacts over the sea birds.
